Data Management & Governance at Contoso

Ensuring data integrity, security, and compliance to power trustworthy decision-making and digital innovation.

Overview

Contoso's data governance strategy ensures high-quality, trusted data assets across the enterprise. We define clear ownership, accountability, and stewardship models to align with regulatory, operational, and analytical needs.

Core Principles

Data Ownership

Each data domain has a designated owner responsible for data quality and compliance.

Data Quality

All data must be accurate, complete, timely, and consistent across systems.

Data Security & Privacy

Data is protected through role-based access, encryption, and compliance with privacy laws.

Accountability

Clear roles and responsibilities are defined across data stewards, custodians, and users.

Data Governance Framework

Our framework spans strategy, processes, people, and technology to embed data governance across the organization.

  • Governance Council: Strategic oversight and policy direction.
  • Data Stewards: Responsible for data quality, lineage, and standards.
  • Policies & Standards: Define acceptable use, quality metrics, and compliance.
  • Metadata Management: Ensures understanding and traceability of data assets.
  • Technology Enablement: Tools and platforms that support data lineage, cataloging, and governance workflows.
